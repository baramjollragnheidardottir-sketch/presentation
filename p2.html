A video for a museum

Kunstmuseum Brandts features an installation of Den fantastiske bus by Jakob Martin Strid. I experienced that there was a lack of promotional material that captured children's attention and sparked their interest in experiencing the children's culture that is so easily accessible at the museum.

With this in mind, I visited the museum to record material for the video. During the editing process, I used Adobe Premiere Pro to edit the footage, arrange the clips in the correct sequence, and add an audio track.

The result was a playful and engaging video that encourages children to visit the museum and explore the book.
