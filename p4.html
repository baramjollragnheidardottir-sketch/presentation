The witch hand

Before
After

Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by the supernatural and the unseen, elements that are also deeply rooted in Icelandic culture. When I came across this image of a witch’s hand, I saw an opportunity to bring subtle life and movement into the composition.

During the project, I began by editing the image in Adobe Photoshop and then transferred the work to Adobe After Effects, where it took on a new form and came to life.

Through small, delicate movements in the fingers, the image invites the viewer to imagine the many possibilities of what the witch might be doing, adding a sense of curiosity to the visual expression.
