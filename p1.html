A Christmas card

Before
After

A client contacted me and asked if I could edit an image she had created by painting the soles of her sons' feet and letting them walk across a canvas. She felt the result was not sufficient and wanted to use the image as a Christmas card. However, achieving the desired result can be challenging when working with children.

After discussing the client's wishes, I began working on the project.

I used Adobe Photoshop to enhance and sharpen the colors, making them more prominent. I also cleaned up unnecessary paint marks and added footprints where paint blobs appeared.

The client was very pleased with the final result and used the image both as a Christmas card and as a keepsake capturing the brothers at a young age.
