Before
After

It is always enjoyable to visit a museum and learn about history and culture, but for young children, the experience becomes even more magical when a painting comes to life. When a painting comes to life, it engages more senses than sight alone, making the experience more immersive and memorable.

During the project, I began by editing the image in Adobe Photoshop and then transferred the work to Adobe After Effects, where it took on a new form and came to life.

The final result presents Van Gogh moving calmly within one of his most famous paintings, a visually engaging and atmospheric experience for audiences of all ages.
